Color-a-Greek-Goddess Image Gallery

Let your imagination go wild with the Color-A-Story Goddess coloring pages. A great activity for the classroom or home. Have your students or children focus on color themes, find out about the history of the Greek goddesses and even make up their own goddess stories.

The Color-A-Story Goddess Coloring Pages are part of a continued effort to incorporate art into the economic development plans of Santa Rosa. The pen-and-ink drawings are proposed sketches for statues that would line the Smart Train Corridor along the planned bicycle path from Cloverdale to San Raphael. The hand=carved life-sized statues would also be placed at rest stops also known as goddess temples. These temples would provide shelter from the elements and a place to meditate. Similar to what would be found in ancient Greece and ancient Rome.
